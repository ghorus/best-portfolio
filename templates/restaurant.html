Our best traditions bring luck, but also pleasure and good taste!

Inspired by local cuisine and Montrealers' favorite dishes, Vallier's chefs know how to evoke good memories with comforting and seasonal dishes.

“Vallier? Like the restaurant on McGill?"" This is the question of the hour for Vallier Dufour. Fun fact: the bistro takes its name from the Montreal restaurateur who, with his partners, imagined a dynamic and warm space where he could entertain like at home.
